Identify Talent Shortage Areas Using Applications vs Job Demand
Business Objective
Detect industries where hiring demand is high but applicant interest is low.

Why This Is Very Valuable
This becomes a workforce planning / HR analytics project.
Employers constantly ask:
“Which sectors are struggling to attract talent?”
You can compare:
Number of job postings
vs 
Number of applications
vs 
Salary attractiveness 

Initial Insights
Some sectors receive high average applications/views:
Social Services 
Banking & Finance 
Human Resources 
Risk Management 
But sectors like:
Engineering 
Construction 
Logistics
may have large job volumes but weaker engagement. 

Potential Business Value
This can uncover:
Talent shortages 
Skills gaps 
Under-attractive industries 
Hiring inefficiencies 
Very useful for:
MOM 
HR consultancies 
Workforce Singapore 
Recruitment firms
